How our exploratory trip works

A Journey into a Treasure Trove of Nature’s Wonders

Our exploratory trips are designed for avid fishermen with a passion for exploration, adventure and discovery.

These expeditions into the Amazon’s heart are tailored to fishermen whose ultimate goal is to venture Where fish have never before seen na angler, or even a lure. We seek out the most untrammeled and untraveled regions of the Amazon Rainforest. If you yearn to be the pioneering angler, casting your lines into untouched waters, then this is the trip for you. Our quests may lead us to seek out giant peacock bass, fanged payara, massive wolfish, acrobatic bicuda and a full range of the Amazon’s renowned giant catfish. More than not, we are left still wondering about what other unknown species may be lurking in these waters, driving our passion for exploration. Group size on our exploratory trips is limited to 8 anglers.

Itinerary / General Information

Departure & Travel - Depending on your departure city, leave home in time to connect with a late afternoon International flight from Miami to Manaus Brazil.

ITINERARY MULTI-SPECIES VARIETY TRIP

- Transfer day & Day 1 Arrive Manaus, Brazil. After clearing customs you will be met by our agent and transferred to the Hotel The next morning, you will be transferred to the airport to board a float plane directly to the Rio guariba.- Relax, enjoy lunch and get your gear ready for an afternoon of fishing.
- Day 2 – Through Day 7 Six full days of fishing. Your angling day begins with a 5:30 AM followed by breakfast at 6:00 AM. Your guide will be ready to depart at first light. Return to camp by dusk for appetizers, drinks and bragging, followed by a delicious dinner. Jungle hikes, spotlighting trips and bird watching can be arranged.
Travel day & Return 10 AM, take floatplane back to Manaus, arriving late morning. You will be provided with a day room at the Hotel and you will have a day and evening in Manaus for relaxing. enjoy great Brazilian restaurants. Late evening, transfer to airport. Depart Ma-naus via International flight for home.
