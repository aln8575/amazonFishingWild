Rio Guariba

Surrounded by spectacular primavel forest, the Rio Guariba is home to a broad swath of the Amazon's incredible biodiversity.

About the Guariba River

In this unique and exclusive fishery, seeks of Nature's untrammeled essence find themselves immersed in a true paradise.
The rio Guariba is the pinnacle of freshwater fishing experiences. With its unparalleled variety, this glorious river provides anglers with the opportunity to catch a lifetime's worth of sought after species - all in one trip. Here, far from the trappings of civilization, sport fishermen can experience the heart-pounding excitement of hand-to-hand combat with the Amazon's incredible gamefish.

The Guariba Lodge

Comfort in the middle of the jungle

Your Rio Guaribe fishing experience is complemented by Guariba Lodge's unexpected "Jungle luxury. Comfortable private cabins or spacious Couple's Suites provide all of the comforts of home - Private bathrooms, hot showers, air-conditioning and unlimited satellite internet "Starlink" are just the beginning.

Accommodations

First class service makes your comfort complete. All of your needs are carefully attended by our team of hospitality professionals.
For those who want even more adventure, you can shed the coddled comfort of the lodge and immerse yourself in the Amazon's pristine environment at our "Satellite Camp". With comfortable tents and and incomparably beautiful settings, you'll not only acess more distant waters, but you'll experience the enchantment of a night in the forest.
